This is an interest calculator for piggyvest's safe lock saving plan.

SafeLock is a fixed savings option that allows you to earn up to 15.5% per annum, usually paid up front. Your Safelock acts as your sub-account so that you can transfer and lock funds from your Piggybank wallet or your debit card for at least 10 days, without access to these funds until maturity. Safelock interests are prorated and paid for the duration you set.
